Poundcake Recipe

By Rayan Master - 25th June, 22 7:00pm

Description

Pound cake is a type of cake traditionally made with a pound of each of four ingredients: flour, butter, eggs, and sugar. Pound cakes are generally baked in either a loaf pan or a Bundt mold. They are sometimes served either dusted with powdered sugar, lightly glazed, or with a coat of icing.

Servings: 8

Time: 15 Minutes

Cook Time: 50 Minutes

Total Time: 1 Hour 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1.3 cups cake flour
  • 0.75 cups sugar
  • 0.75 tsp salt
  • 13 tbsps butter softened

Steps

  1. Heat Oven to 350f, grease loaf pan with butter
  2. Im Medium Bowl whisk together milk, eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix Flour, Sugar, baking powder and salt with an electric mixer.
  4. Incorporate Butter and half of the egg mixture
  5. Scrape down sides and add remaining egg mixture, beating for 30 seconds.
  6. Scrape batter into pan and bake for 50 - 55 minutes, until toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Place cake to cool for 10 mins on wire rack.
  8. Enjoy!
